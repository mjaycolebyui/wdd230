TARGET AUDIENCE

The target audience of the site will be business owners, workers, and the general public that is interested in the commerce of the city.

PERSONAS

JULIA

Julia is 35 years old, and after getting her master's degree in business and working in an office, she's taken the plunge and started her own company. Wanting the business to boom so she can provide for her husband and kids, she is constantly up-to-date on her city's chamber of commerce page, looking for when meetings occur and different ways she can build her network.

Steve

Steve is a boots-to-the-floor type of business man, with 20 years if owning his business. He has been involved in the chamber of commerce for the past 10 years, and testifies of its ability to increase his network and business. Now, he is actively trying to promote other local businesses, and keeps tabs on his status in the chamber on his mobile device wherever he goes.

SCENARIOS

  • How can I get more involved?
  • What businesses are actively participating?
  • What are the membership levels?
  • What is the history of the city's chamber of commerce?
  • How do I contact the city's chamber of commerce?
