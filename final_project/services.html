Suites

The Celestial

The best hotel suite available, our Celestial rooms are top-level comfort for families of all sizes. Three bedrooms, two bathrooms, kitchen, and dining area all make a longer stay more comfortable.

The Terrestrial

Only a step down from the Celestial, the Terrestrial rooms are for those who may not be willing to pay the same price as the Celestial ones. Two bedrooms, one bathroom, and small kitchen appliances.

The Telestial

This room is for those who want the bare minimum. Although still a hotel room at the Temple Inn and Suites, this is for those who will be traveling solo. One bedreoom, one bathroom.
